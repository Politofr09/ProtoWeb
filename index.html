Welcome to My Game Engine

Proto aims at being customizable, extensible and giving the user the ability to control their engine to suit their needs

Info

Proto is a game engine I made for personal use and experimenting with graphics programming. It is currently in a very beta state but I plan on adding lots of features. Currently it supports 2D, 3D, and text rendering.
